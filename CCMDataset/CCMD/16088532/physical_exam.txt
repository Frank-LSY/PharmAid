ADMISSION PHYSICAL EXAM
========================
GENERAL: Lying in bed, arouses to voice, appears diaphoretic and
uncomfortable
HEENT: AT/NC, anicteric sclera, MMM, no oropharyngeal edema or
exudate
NECK: supple, no LAD
CV: RRR, S1/S2, no murmurs, gallops, or rubs
PULM: CTAB, no wheezes, rales, rhonchi, breathing comfortably
without use of accessory muscles
GI: abdomen soft, nondistended, nontender in all quadrants, no
rebound/guarding, no hepatosplenomegaly
BACK: TTP in right paraspinal muscles, no vertebral tenderness 
EXTREMITIES: no cyanosis, clubbing, or edema
PULSES: 2+ radial pulses bilaterally
NEURO: Alert, moving all 4 extremities with purpose, face
symmetric
DERM: warm and well perfused, no excoriations or lesions, no
rashes
